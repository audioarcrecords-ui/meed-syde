About

Meed / Syde is a techno producer operating at the intersection of machine precision and raw human energy. The project exists to craft soundscapes that move bodies and alter states — music designed for dark rooms, thunderous soundsystems, and the hours where time dissolves.

Releasing on Audio Arc, every track carries an identity rooted in intensity: driving rhythms that lock in and never let go, textures that crawl under the skin, and a production philosophy that values impact over excess.

From the ominous roll of The Lurker to the unrelenting assault of Final Judgement, Meed / Syde builds worlds within tracks — dense, immersive, and built to hit hardest at 4AM when the crowd has become one.

With 19,000+ monthly Spotify listeners and a rapidly expanding catalog, the project is a growing force in the global techno underground.
